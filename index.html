[ ORDINARY PERSON ] ?

PLAYER

LEVEL 1 ?
Your Title
Your title reflects how the world would recognise someone with your current attribute levels. Everyone begins as an Ordinary Person — not as an insult, but as an honest starting point. As you complete quests and your attributes grow, your title will change to reflect the person you are becoming.
Your Level
Your level is calculated from the average of all five core attributes. It starts at 1 and rises as you consistently complete quests and grow. A higher level means your attributes are broadly stronger across the board. Think of it less as a rank and more as a reflection of how much deliberate effort you have put in.
STRENGTH ?
10
Strength
Represents your physical capability — your energy, fitness, and ability to exert yourself in the world. A score of 10 is your starting baseline: you are functional and alive. As you complete Strength quests, this grows toward 100, which would represent peak physical vitality and consistency. This number is not a precise measurement — it is a guided reflection of the effort you are putting into your physical self.
INTELLIGENCE ?
10
Intelligence
Represents your capacity to learn, reason, and make sound decisions. A score of 10 means you have a working mind and the ability to grow. Completing Intelligence quests — reading, reflecting, solving problems — pushes this toward 100, which would reflect someone who is constantly learning and thinking deliberately. This is not an IQ score. It is a measure of your intellectual engagement.
AGILITY ?
10
Agility
Represents your adaptability — how well you respond to change, disruption, and the unexpected. A score of 10 means you can function when life shifts. Growing this toward 100 means you become someone who navigates change with ease and finds opportunity in disruption. Agility quests train you to break patterns and respond rather than react.
ENDURANCE ?
10
Endurance
Represents your ability to sustain effort over time — physically, mentally, and emotionally. A score of 10 means you can get through a day. Growing this toward 100 means you become someone who persists through difficulty without burning out. Sleep, consistency, and finishing what you start are the foundations of this attribute.
CHARISMA ?
10
Charisma
Represents your social presence — how you connect with, influence, and build relationships with others. A score of 10 means you are capable of human connection. Growing this toward 100 means you become someone people are drawn to, trust, and want to collaborate with. Charisma quests build your network and deepen your ability to communicate genuinely.
LUCK ?
10
Luck
Luck is a derived attribute — you cannot raise it directly. It is calculated automatically as the average of all your other attributes. This is intentional. Research shows that what people experience as luck is largely the result of preparation, awareness, resilience, and strong relationships. Grow your core attributes and your luck rises with them.
